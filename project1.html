Feminist theory sees a connection between all kinds of violence and worldwide security to local matters.
Through the DB for women project, we're striving to build an environment where women can exist fearlessly. We've created an app targeting young and susceptible women who depend on public transportation, allowing them to map out secure paths based on the reviews of other women and identify safe areas.
The app mitigates fear for the users and their community, and it incorporates an emergency panel for immediate communication, incident documentation, and evidence collection.

The emergency board on the app's home screen provides a comprehensive set of features designed to enhance personal safety and expedite emergency response.

From triggering emergency audio recordings to quick access to emergency contacts, streamlined reporting, group travel facilitation, and location sharing, these features empower users to take proactive measures and seek assistance when needed, fostering a greater sense of security in potentially dangerous situations.
