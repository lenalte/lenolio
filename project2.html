Symposium is an innovative party planning platform that revolutionizes the way you organize and coordinate your events. This website offers a wide range of features designed to simplify every aspect of party planning, from start to finish.

One of the standout features is "Less is More", which allows your guests to effortlessly track their financial contributions to the party. This transparency enables everyone to see how much they have contributed and helps determine who should stay behind to assist with post-party cleanup.

Furthermore, Symposium goes beyond party planning by fostering connections among guests. If you receive an invitation to a Symposium-hosted party, you gain access to a list of other invitees. This enables you to reach out and coordinate carpooling arrangements, making transportation logistics a breeze.

Another valuable feature offered by Symposium is the "shopping list." This feature simplifies the process of procuring party essentials by allowing the party planner to compile a list of required items. The list can then be shared with the invited guests, enabling everyone to select and commit to bringing specific items of their choice to the party. The flexibility of this feature allows participants to decide how many items they are willing to provide.

Once a guest clicks on an item from the shopping list, it is automatically marked as taken and added to their personal shopping list. This ensures that other participants no longer see the item as available since it has already been claimed.

The shopping list feature plays a significant role in supporting the "Less is More" functionality as it assists in calculating each participant's financial contribution by tracking the items selected from the list.

Furthermore, Symposium provides a seamless experience for creating and sending invitations directly through the website.

Say goodbye to the hassle of traditional invitation methods; now you can effortlessly plan your party and send out invitations in one convenient location.
