This app revolutionizes how you calculate and visualize your CO2 footprint. By completing the initial questionnaire, you can determine your average carbon emissions and compare them to global and national averages.

Additionally, the ability to connect with your car provides further insights into your transportation-related carbon emissions, fostering an eco-conscious mindset and facilitating a greener lifestyle.

Another noteworthy feature of the app is the ability to browse through a wealth of extra tips on how to lead a more climate-friendly lifestyle. With this feature, you can explore a variety of resources and recommendations to enhance your environmental consciousness and make sustainable choices.
