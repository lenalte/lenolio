Smart Tutoring is an online platform designed to connect students with tutors who perfectly align with their educational requirements.

This exceptional feature is facilitated through comprehensive questionnaires that both students and tutors are required to complete.

These questionnaires enable an algorithm to carefully match the ideal tutor to each student, resulting in a tailored and highly effective learning experience.

The questionnaires in Smart Tutoring cover crucial aspects such as language proficiency and subject expertise to facilitate the ideal tutor-student matches.

The platform thrives on the generosity of tutors who volunteer their time, making education more accessible and equitable for all.
